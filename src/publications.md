Below we list publications that directly relate to Fram.

* **Deciding not to Decide:
  Sound and Complete Effect Inference in the Presence of
  Higher-Rank Polymorphism.**  
  Patrycja Balik, Szymon Jędras, and Piotr Polesiuk.  
  *ESOP 2026*, Turin, Italy, April 2026.  
  [(appendix)](https://github.com/fram-lang/fram-papers/releases/download/esop26/esop26-appendix.pdf)
  [(artifact)](https://doi.org/10.5281/zenodo.18197463)

  This paper presents the algorithm for effect inference used in Fram,
  along with Rocq proofs of the algorithm's soundness and completeness.

* **Fram: Named Parameters Pushed to the Limit.** (extended abstract)  
  Patrycja Balik and Piotr Polesiuk.  
  *ML Family Workshop 2024*, Milan, Italy, September 2024.  
  [(pdf)](https://ii.uni.wroc.pl/~ppolesiuk/papers/ml2024.pdf)
  [(talk)](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=TQUvXy547AA)

  An extended abstract and talk showcasing Fram's named parameter mechanism.
